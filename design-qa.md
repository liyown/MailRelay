## Review

- Composition: passed — all ten sections remain, while shorter introductions and scenario copy create clearer pauses between proof surfaces.
- Typography: passed — oversized editorial headings, compact mono labels, readable body copy, and strong hierarchy remain consistent from desktop through mobile.
- Color and surface: passed — warm paper background, restrained coral accent, dark terminal/security surfaces, and hairline separators preserve the reference direction without copying its product identity.
- Responsive behavior: passed — 390 px viewport has no document overflow; scenarios, proof grids, comparison content, and CTA collapse into a readable single-column sequence.
- Product fidelity: passed — golden scenarios, Discovery, comparison, runtime trust claims, handler maturity, and quick start remain intact without repeated marketing claims.
- Editorial quality: passed — conversational qualifiers and duplicate promises were removed; each section now carries one claim and one supporting sentence.
- Interaction: passed — anchor navigation, documentation links, GitHub link, and final quick-start links remain visible and usable.

No raster illustration assets were needed; the product story is demonstrated with native HTML/CSS email and terminal artifacts.
